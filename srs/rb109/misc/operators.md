## ternary operator
`?:`
conditional statement ? first possible outcome : second possible outcome

```
if "chocolate".size > 4 ? 
  "More than four characters"
else 
  "Less than four characters"
end
```
becomes
`"chocolate".size > 4 ? "More than four characters" : "Less than four characters"`
